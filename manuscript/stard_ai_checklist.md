# STARD-AI-Oriented Checklist

- Study objective and intended use stated.
- Dataset sources, public-data scope, and MIMIC/API exclusion stated.
- Eligibility and sampling strategy specified.
- Reference standard and scoring rubric specified.
- Primary and secondary outcomes separated.
- Model identities, versions, dates, prompts, and parameters recorded in inference output schema.
- Missing, ambiguous, and contradictory evidence perturbations documented.
- Human review and adjudication plan specified.
- Statistical methods and confidence intervals specified.
- Limitations and non-deployment boundary stated.
