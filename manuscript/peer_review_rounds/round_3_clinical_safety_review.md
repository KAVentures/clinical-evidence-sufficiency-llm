# Round 3: Clinical Safety Review

## Major concerns

- Unsafe overconfidence is clinically plausible only when missing or contradictory evidence is genuinely necessary for the requested diagnosis or recommendation.
- Treatment-harm labels should not be treated as final without clinician review.

## Minor concerns

- General safety-net advice should not be penalized as overconfident management.

## Required fixes applied

- Manuscript and protocol state that LLM judge labels are triage labels, not clinical ground truth.
- Annotation export supports blinded clinician review and comments.
