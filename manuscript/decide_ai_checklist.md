# DECIDE-AI-Oriented Checklist

- Clinical decision-support context stated.
- Human-in-the-loop role preserved.
- Safety risks from overconfident recommendations specified.
- Retrospective benchmark nature stated.
- No autonomous-care or patient-outcome claims made.
- Prospective validation identified as future work.
- Data governance and restricted-data boundaries stated.
- MIMIC-derived data excluded from external API execution.
- Failure modes and residual risk documented.
